# Enterprise Support

Tairitsu offers enterprise-grade support for organizations deploying production applications.

## Support Levels

### Community Support (Free)

- Public documentation and guides
- Community Discord and GitHub Discussions
- Bug fixes via public releases
- Best practices documentation

### Professional Support

For organizations requiring dedicated support, contact us for:

- Priority bug fixes and security patches
- Direct access to core developers
- Custom integration assistance
- Architecture review and guidance
- SLA-backed response times

### Enterprise Support

Enterprise licenses include:

- 24/7 critical issue support
- Dedicated support engineer
- On-site training and workshops
- Custom feature development
- Long-term support (LTS) versions
- Security audit assistance

## SLA (Service Level Agreement)

### Professional Support

| Severity | Response Time | Resolution Time |
|----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Critical (P1) | 4 hours | 2 business days |
| High (P2) | 1 business day | 5 business days |
| Medium (P3) | 2 business days | 2 weeks |
| Low (P4) | 1 week | Best effort |

### Enterprise Support

| Severity | Response Time | Resolution Time |
|----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Critical (P1) | 1 hour | 24 hours |
| High (P2) | 4 hours | 2 business days |
| Medium (P3) | 1 business day | 1 week |
| Low (P4) | 2 business days | 2 weeks |

## Severity Definitions

### P1 - Critical
- Production system completely down
- Data loss or corruption
- Security vulnerability with active exploit

### P2 - High
- Major feature not working
- Significant performance degradation
- Workarounds available but impact productivity

### P3 - Medium
- Minor feature issues
- Inconvenient workarounds required
- Documentation errors

### P4 - Low
- Cosmetic issues
- Feature requests
- General questions

## Long-Term Support (LTS)

LTS versions receive:

- 2 years of bug fixes
- 3 years of security updates
- Backports for critical issues
- Stable API guarantees

Current LTS versions: None (first LTS: v1.0.0)
